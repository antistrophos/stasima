# The disposition layer

The always-on stances that govern every act regardless of dock or verb — loaded cold with the arrival road. Under Aous there are five: reconcile-before, fluency-is-the-risk (both directions — inflation *and* deflation), reciprocal vulnerability, errors-are-instructions, trust-the-server. Scope: they govern how a seat interacts with canon and the substrate — never a doctrine of how to *be*.

- **Tools wielded:** none directly — the layer pervades; each stance's mechanics live in the dock that wields them.
- **Skill encoding:** [`skills/aous/SKILL.md`](../../skills/aous/SKILL.md)
- **Canon sources:** one entry per stance at `meta/dispositions/*` in your deployment's canon, named by the suite manifest (`meta/suites/aous.md`) — single home per stance; the encoding points, never copies.
- **Design record:** the orthogonality re-pass (which stances survive a river-change untouched) and the deflation companion (found by the first cold seat) live in the reference practice's canon.
